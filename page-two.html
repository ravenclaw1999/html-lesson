Before we begin, you need to know what a tag is in html.

The purpose of these tags is to organize the content on our site.

There are many tags in HTML, but let's review some common tags.

The example shown above is a paragraph tag.



Another tag is the "a" tag, which is for links.

At the top of every HTML page, you need to include this:

Next, you will include the <html> tags.

Inside the <html> tags, you will include the <head> and <body> tags.

The <head> tags contain the title of your webpage and other information about your website.

The <body> tags contain the actual content of your website.

There are also a set of tags called the h tags.

There are 6 of these tags. It ranges from smallest to

largest starting with <h1> and ending with <h6>


<h1>I am the largest heading</h1>

<h2>I am a large heading</h2>

<h3>I am a smaller heading</h3>

<h>I am a even smaller heading</h4>

<h5>I am a much smaller heading</h5>
<h6>I am the smallest heading</h6>

Note: Attributes can also be added to these tags. Attributes tell us additional things about a tag.

There is also something called comments.

They are simply for you to leave notes for yourself and other coders.

They are not displayed in the browser. Whatever code you comment out will not execute.
